About Us

Brainmaven is a Toronto-based company focused on building AI-driven prediction models for human flourishing. Brainmaven is led by Dr. Ajay Agrawal. Ajay Agrawal is the Geoffrey Taber Chair in Entrepreneurship and Innovation and Professor of Strategic Management at the University of Toronto’s Rotman School of Management. He is the founder of the Creative Destruction Lab (CDL), and co-founder of NEXT Canada. He is also the co-author of the bestselling book "Prediction Machines: The Simple Economics of Artificial Intelligence".
